AI Dependency Survey Study

Conducted a quantitative survey study on AI dependency among university students, focusing on how usage motivations, psychological beliefs, trust in AI, and verification habits relate to reliance on generative AI tools. The final hierarchical regression model explained approximately 65% of the variance in AI dependency, with perceived performance improvement and trust in AI over instructors emerging as key predictors.
